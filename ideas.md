<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Brutalist Modernism
  **Core Principles**:
  1. Unapologetic Boldness: Large, heavy typography and high-contrast elements.
  2. Raw Authenticity: Embracing the "behind the scenes" nature of media production.
  3. Structural Clarity: Grid-breaking layouts that guide the eye dynamically.
  4. Impact over Decoration: Every element serves a purpose; no fluff.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Dark Gray Backgrounds (#1a1a1a)**: Provides a cinematic, studio-like atmosphere.
  - **Electric Blue (#007bff)**: Represents technology, clarity, and the "IQ" aspect.
  - **Vibrant Orange (#ff6600)**: Represents creativity, energy, and the "Reel" aspect.
  - *Reasoning*: High contrast against dark backgrounds creates a sense of urgency and modernity, fitting for a social media agency.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - Asymmetric grids with overlapping elements.
  - Large, full-width imagery breaking the container bounds.
  - Sticky scrolling sections for storytelling.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Oversized Typography**: Headlines that take up 50% of the viewport height.
  - **Visible Grid Lines**: Subtle, thin lines hinting at the structure (like a camera viewfinder).
  - **Monochrome Image Filters with Hover Color**: Images start black & white (or duotone) and reveal full color on hover.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Micro-interactions**: Buttons that "glitch" or shift on hover.
  - **Scroll-triggered reveals**: Content slides in from unexpected angles.
  - **Cursor effects**: A custom cursor that changes state over interactive elements (e.g., "Play" icon over videos).

  **Animation**:
  - **Kinetic Typography**: Text that moves, scales, or marquees on scroll.
  - **Parallax Scrolling**: Background elements move at different speeds than foreground content.
  - **Sharp Transitions**: No soft fades; cuts and slides, mimicking video editing cuts.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headlines**: *Teko* or *Oswald* (Condensed, bold, industrial).
  - **Body**: *Roboto Mono* or *Space Mono* (Technical, script-like, organized).
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Swiss International Style (Dark Mode)
  **Core Principles**:
  1. Mathematical Precision: Everything aligns perfectly to a grid.
  2. Objective Photography: Images are presented without filters, letting the content speak.
  3. Typography as Image: Type is treated as a major visual element.
  4. Minimalist Boldness: Less is more, but make it loud.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Matte Dark Gray (#222222)**: Sophisticated, print-like quality.
  - **International Orange (#ff5500)**: Used strictly for calls to action and highlights.
  - **Cool Blue (#0055ff)**: Used for links and secondary information.
  - *Reasoning*: Timeless, professional, and trustworthy. It says "we know what we're doing."

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - Modular grid system (12-column).
  - Split-screen layouts (50/50 text and image).
  - ample negative space to let content breathe.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Huge Horizontal Rules**: Thick lines separating sections.
  - **Numbered Lists**: Large, stylized numbers for steps or services.
  - **Left-Aligned Everything**: Strict adherence to the grid.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Instant Response**: Buttons and links react immediately (no laggy animations).
  - **Clean Hovers**: Simple color inversions or underlines.
  - **Focus States**: Clear indicators of where the user is.

  **Animation**:
  - **Staggered Reveals**: Content loads line by line.
  - **Simple Slides**: Images slide in from the side.
  - **No Distractions**: Animation is used only to guide, not to entertain.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headlines**: *Helvetica Now Display* or *Archivo* (Neutral, bold, classic).
  - **Body**: *Helvetica Now Text* or *Roboto* (Neutral, legible).
</idea>
